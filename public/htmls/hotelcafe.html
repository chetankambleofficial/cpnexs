~ our story ~

A calm corner on 17th C Main Road

Cafe Medley sits in KHB Colony, Koramangala 5th Block — easy to find on the main road, and easy to park a two-wheeler outside. It's the kind of place people stumble on while looking for somewhere quiet to unwind, and end up adding to their regular rotation.

The room is warm without trying hard, the staff remember your order by the second visit, and the menu does comfort food properly — from sizzlers to pancakes to a cheesecake people keep photographing before they eat it.
